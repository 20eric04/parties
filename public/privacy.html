Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Parties Inc. ("Parties," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Parties app and website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe
  • Booking preferences and history
  • Communications with our concierge team

We also collect certain information automatically, such as device type, IP address, and usage analytics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process bookings, communicate reservation confirmations, improve our services, and send you relevant updates about venues and events in your selected cities. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

Payment Security

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or sensitive financial data on our servers.

Data Sharing

We share booking details with venue partners only to the extent necessary to fulfill your reservation. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@parties.app.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at privacy@parties.app.
